About Us

Our Purpose

Heart disease is a serious problem in Australia. Our purpose is to reduce and prevent the heart disease.

  1. Reducing the death caused by heart disease
  2. Making people pay more attention to their heart
  3. Improving people's healthy of heart
  4. Teaching people more first aids skills
  5. Seting up a place for patients

What we do

For recent years, heart disease has become one of problems thearten Australian's life. According to our survey, most people lack knowledge of heart and first aids skills. Few people know any organization of heart.

Therefore, our group want to build an orginazation which could provid enough information and and preventive skills. In addition, other patients could come here to have discussions.

Cardiovascular disease

The number of death toll caused by Cardiovascular disease is the largest number in world. Especially in Australia, one Australian could be killed every 12 mintues due to heart disease.
